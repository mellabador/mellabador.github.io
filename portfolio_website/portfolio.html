    Girls Who Code (Adobe NYC)

    Through Girls Who Code (GWCs), I learnt a lot of the fundamentals of coding and met new people from different backgrounds and skills. It started simply with simple Scratch and later throughout the weeks we expanded our knowledge to HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and may more. It was really challenging having to go in with little experience and trying to jump into coding. I had always thought coding was a very boring aspect but there are actually many branches of computer science. My outlook of computer science has shifted throughout the weeks as I realize how complex coding is to how many things could be achieved through coding. I was also able to listen to people's stories and their journey in the field they are in. Many of the guest speakers made me develop a new profound respect to those who know how to code because they are helping technology evole from where it is at now. Their stories and advice was empowering and enlightening. I defientely want to reconsider taking courses in computer science in college as well as creating a coding club for my last year in high school.
